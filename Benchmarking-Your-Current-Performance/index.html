Gaming Performance Benchmark

To assess your game's current performance, follow these steps:

  • Launch the game you wish to benchmark.
  • Check if the game has a built-in FPS counter in its settings or options. If not, you can use third-party software like MSI Afterburner or FRAPS.
  • Play the game for approximately 10-15 minutes while monitoring the FPS counter.
  • Take note of the average FPS and any noticeable lag or stuttering during gameplay.

System Boot Time Measurement

The time it takes for your PC to boot up is an essential metric. To measure it accurately:

  • Restart your PC.
  • Use a stopwatch or a smartphone timer app to record the boot process's duration.
  • Note down the time taken for your PC to fully boot into the desktop.

Application Load Time Assessment

Application load times can significantly impact your day-to-day experience. To measure them:

  • Launch the application or game you want to benchmark.
  • Time how long it takes for the application to load and be ready for use.
  • Record the application load time.
